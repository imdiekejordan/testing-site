OUR PROCESS

Your Retrofit Journey with SoCal Seismic

Pre-Retrofit

You will have 12 weeks (or 90 days) to gather pre-retrofit photos and documents, and to either hire a contractor from EBB or select SoCal Seismic for your project.

Retrofit

After EBB has approved your uploaded pre-retrofit documentation, you will have six months from that approval date to complete your retrofit with our expert team.

Post-Retrofit

Within 30-days after completion of the retrofit, items must be submitted to EBB for payment using the online Homeowner Dashboard. We'll guide you through this process.

Payment Processing

Incentive payments typically will be mailed within four weeks following final approval by EBB. Our team ensures all documentation meets EBB requirements for timely processing.
