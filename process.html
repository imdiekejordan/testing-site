Process

Our Process

Stage 1

Plan

We begin by collaborating closely with you to understand your vision, budget, and site conditions. This step ensures your timber frame project has a solid foundation, considering factors like land characteristics, zoning regulations, and structural needs. By aligning every detail upfront, we set the stage for a smooth, efficient process, minimizing potential challenges down the road

Stage 2

Design

Our team provides expert guidance through the entire design phase, covering timber frame layouts, structural insulated panel (SIP) integration, and consulting on key architectural elements. We connect you with trusted architects and engineers to refine your ideas and develop a comprehensive blueprint that balances aesthetics, functionality, and efficiency.

Stage 3

Production

Using premium-quality materials, we precision-cut and hand-finish each timber component in our workshop, ensuring both durability and craftsmanship. Every beam and joint is meticulously prepared to guarantee a seamless fit during assembly. This controlled production process enhances structural integrity, reduces waste, and ensures a faster, more efficient build on-site.

Stage 4

Build

Once on-site, our expert team carefully assembles your timber frame with skill and precision, ensuring a structurally sound and visually striking result. We coordinate every aspect of the installation, from raising the frame to final detailing, guaranteeing a seamless construction experience. The end result is a beautifully crafted, long-lasting timber frame structure built to stand the test of time.

What sets us apart from other timber frame companies?

Unlike many timber frame companies that only supply the wood or handle frame installation, we are a full-service company. We guide you through every step — from custom design and precise craftsmanship to frame raising and full construction support. Our team ensures your project is seamless, combining beautiful timber frames with expert project management to bring your vision to life.

What area does Lake Superior Timber Frames serve?

We proudly serve Minnesota and Wisconsin, offering custom timber frame solutions for homes, cabins, barns, and commercial projects. No matter where you are, we’re ready to bring your dream timber frame structure to life.

Do we handle full construction?

Yes, we offer full construction services or collaborate with your builder to ensure the timber frame is seamlessly integrated into your project. From design and fabrication to raising the frame, we provide expert support every step of the way.

How long does a timber frame project take to complete?

A timber frame project varies in length depending on the design complexity, site preparation, and weather conditions. The timber frame itself can be erected in just a few days or weeks, speeding up the overall construction process.
