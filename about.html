FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long has Lyne Construction been in business?

Lyne Construction has been serving the community for over 7 years, building a reputation for quality workmanship and reliable service.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, we are fully licensed (LIC: 1037488) and insured. We carry comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage to protect our clients and team members.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the San Francisco, CA area and surrounding communities. Contact us to confirm if we service your specific location.

How do I get a quote for my project?

You can request a quote through our website, by phone, or by email. We'll schedule a consultation to discuss your project needs and provide a detailed estimate.

What types of projects do you handle?

We offer a wide range of professional services including electrical work, plumbing, roofing, foundation repairs, window installation, kitchen and bathroom renovations, deck building, and tile services. Our team is also equipped to handle custom projects tailored to your unique needs—no job is too big, too small, or too specific. Whether you're looking for standard home improvements or one-of-a-kind solutions, we're here to bring your vision to life with quality craftsmanship and attention to detail.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes, we stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties. The specific warranty terms will be detailed in your contract, and we'll ensure you understand all coverage details before we begin your project.
