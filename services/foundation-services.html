FAQ

Foundation Services FAQs

What foundation services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive foundation services including repairs, waterproofing, crack repairs, foundation leveling, basement waterproofing, and structural assessments. Our experienced team handles both residential and commercial foundation projects.

How do I know if I have foundation problems?

Common signs of foundation problems include cracks in walls or floors, doors that won't close properly, uneven floors, gaps around windows, and water in the basement. We can perform a thorough inspection to assess your foundation's condition.

Do you offer emergency foundation services?

Yes, we provide emergency foundation services for urgent situations. Whether you're dealing with severe cracks, water intrusion, or structural concerns, our team is available to respond quickly and protect your home from further damage.

What methods do you use for foundation repair?

We use various foundation repair methods including piering, slabjacking, wall anchors, and carbon fiber reinforcement. The best method depends on your specific situation, which we'll determine during our thorough inspection.

How long does foundation repair take?

Foundation repair duration varies based on the extent of damage and chosen repair method. Simple repairs might take a few days, while major projects could take several weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

Do you offer warranties on foundation repairs?

Yes, we offer comprehensive warranties on our foundation repairs. The specific warranty terms will be detailed in your contract, and we'll ensure you understand all coverage details before we begin your project.
