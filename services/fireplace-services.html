FAQ

Fireplace FAQs

What fireplace services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive fireplace services including new installations, renovations, repairs, maintenance, and safety inspections. Our services cover both traditional wood-burning and modern gas fireplaces, as well as custom designs to match your home's style.

Are your fireplace services licensed and insured?

Yes, we are fully licensed (LIC: 1037488) and insured. We are bonded through the contractor state license board (CSLB) and carry comprehensive liability insurance coverage to protect our clients during all fireplace installations and renovations.

How do I know if my fireplace needs renovation?

Signs that your fireplace may need renovation include visible cracks, smoke issues, outdated design, poor efficiency, or if it hasn't been maintained in several years. We can perform a thorough inspection to assess your fireplace's condition and recommend necessary improvements.

Do you offer emergency fireplace repairs?

Yes, we provide emergency fireplace repair services. If you're experiencing issues like smoke backdraft, structural concerns, or other safety hazards, our team is available to respond quickly and address the problem safely.

What is the typical cost for fireplace services?

Fireplace service costs vary based on the project scope, materials, and type of fireplace. We provide detailed, transparent quotes before starting any work. Contact us for a free estimate tailored to your specific needs.

How long does a fireplace installation or renovation take?

Project duration depends on the complexity and scope of work. Simple repairs might take a few hours, while complete installations or major renovations could take several days. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation and work efficiently to minimize disruption.
