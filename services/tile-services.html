FAQ

Tile Services FAQs

What types of tile installation services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive tile installation services including ceramic, porcelain, natural stone, glass, and mosaic tiles. Our services cover floors, walls, backsplashes, showers, and outdoor areas. We handle both new installations and repairs.

How long does a typical tile installation take?

The duration of a tile installation project depends on the size of the area, type of tile, and complexity of the design. A standard bathroom floor might take 1-2 days, while a full kitchen backsplash could be completed in a day. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

Do you provide tile repair services?

Yes, we offer tile repair services including fixing cracked or loose tiles, replacing damaged tiles, regrouting, and addressing water damage issues. We can match existing tiles and ensure repairs blend seamlessly with your current installation.

What should I consider when choosing tiles?

Consider factors such as the room's purpose, traffic levels, moisture exposure, and your desired aesthetic. We'll help you choose tiles that are both beautiful and practical for your specific space, considering durability, maintenance requirements, and slip resistance.

Do you offer tile design consultation?

Yes, we provide design consultation services to help you select the perfect tiles, patterns, and layouts for your space. We can create custom designs, suggest complementary materials, and help you achieve your desired look while staying within your budget.

What is your warranty policy for tile installations?

We stand behind our work with a comprehensive warranty that covers installation quality and workmanship. The specific warranty terms will be detailed in your contract, and we'll ensure you understand all coverage details before we begin your project.
