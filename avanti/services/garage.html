FAQ

Garage Conversion FAQs

What garage conversion services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive garage conversion services including living space conversion, ADU conversion, home office conversion, gym conversion, and complete garage remodeling. We handle everything from design to final inspection.

How long does a garage conversion take?

A typical garage conversion takes 4-8 weeks, depending on the scope and complexity. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation and work efficiently to minimize disruption.

Do you handle permits and inspections?

Yes, we handle all necessary permits, zoning requirements, and coordinate with local authorities throughout the conversion process. We ensure all work meets local building codes and regulations.

What types of garage conversions can you do?

We can convert garages into various living spaces including bedrooms, home offices, gyms, entertainment rooms, guest suites, and complete ADUs. We'll help you choose the best option for your needs.

Do you offer design services?

Yes, we offer professional design services for garage conversions. Our designers will help you create a functional and beautiful space that meets your needs and preferences.

What is your warranty policy?

We offer comprehensive warranties on both materials and workmanship for all garage conversion projects. The specific warranty terms will be detailed in your contract.
