FAQ

ADU Construction FAQs

What ADU services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive ADU services including new construction, garage conversions, basement conversions, attic conversions, and complete ADU design and build services. We handle everything from initial design to final inspection.

How long does an ADU project take?

ADU projects typically take 3-6 months from design to completion, depending on the scope and complexity. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation and keep you updated throughout the process.

Do you handle permits and zoning requirements?

Yes, we handle all necessary permits, zoning requirements, and coordinate with local authorities throughout the ADU process. We ensure all work meets local building codes and ADU regulations.

What types of ADUs can you build?

We can build various types of ADUs including detached units, attached units, garage conversions, basement conversions, and attic conversions. We'll help you choose the best option for your property and needs.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we can help you explore financing options for your ADU project. We work with various lenders and can provide guidance on ADU-specific financing programs available in your area.

What is your warranty policy?

We offer comprehensive warranties on both materials and workmanship for all ADU projects. The specific warranty terms will be detailed in your contract, and we'll ensure you understand all coverage details.
