FAQ

Framing Services FAQs

What framing services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive framing services including wall framing, roof framing, floor framing, structural framing, metal framing, and complete building framing. We handle both residential and commercial construction projects.

How long does a framing project take?

Framing projects typically take 1-4 weeks, depending on the scope and complexity. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation and work efficiently to meet your project deadlines.

Do you handle permits and inspections?

Yes, we handle all necessary permits, engineering requirements, and coordinate with inspectors throughout the framing process. We ensure all work meets local building codes and structural standards.

What types of framing can you do?

We can handle various types of framing including wood framing, metal framing, structural steel framing, roof trusses, floor joists, wall systems, and complete building envelope framing for all construction types.

Do you offer structural engineering support?

Yes, we work with structural engineers to ensure all framing meets engineering requirements and building codes. We coordinate engineering plans and ensure proper structural integrity for all projects.

What is your warranty policy?

We offer comprehensive warranties on all framing work and structural components. The specific warranty terms will be detailed in your contract, and we'll ensure you understand all coverage details.
