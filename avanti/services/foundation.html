FAQ

Foundation Services FAQs

What foundation services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive foundation services including new foundation construction, foundation repair, foundation inspection, waterproofing, structural reinforcement, and complete foundation restoration. We handle both residential and commercial projects.

How long does a foundation project take?

Foundation projects typically take 2-6 weeks, depending on the scope and complexity. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation and work efficiently to ensure structural integrity.

Do you handle permits and inspections?

Yes, we handle all necessary permits, engineering requirements, and coordinate with inspectors throughout the foundation process. We ensure all work meets local building codes and structural standards.

What types of foundations can you build?

We can build various types of foundations including concrete slab foundations, pier and beam foundations, basement foundations, crawl space foundations, and specialized foundations for different soil conditions.

Do you offer foundation repair services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive foundation repair services including crack repair, settling correction, waterproofing, structural reinforcement, and complete foundation restoration for damaged foundations.

What is your warranty policy?

We offer comprehensive warranties on all foundation work and structural components. The specific warranty terms will be detailed in your contract, and we'll ensure you understand all coverage details.
