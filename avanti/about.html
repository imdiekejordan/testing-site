FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve in San Diego?

We serve the entire San Diego area, including San Diego, Chula Vista, National City, La Mesa, El Cajon, and surrounding communities. Our team is licensed and insured to work throughout California.

How long does a typical renovation project take?

Project timelines vary depending on the scope and complexity. A kitchen renovation typically takes 4-6 weeks, bathroom renovations 2-4 weeks, and whole home renovations 8-16 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes! We offer free, no-obligation consultations and estimates for all projects. Our team will visit your home, discuss your vision, and provide a detailed quote with transparent pricing.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. Avanti Construction is fully licensed (LIC #1125752) and insured. We carry comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage to protect our clients and team members.

What types of projects do you specialize in?

We specialize in residential construction and renovation, including kitchen and bathroom remodels, home additions, ADU construction, garage conversions, foundation work, framing, and grading services. We handle projects of all sizes.

How do I get started with my project?

Getting started is easy! Simply contact us through our website or call us directly. We'll schedule a free consultation to discuss your project, answer questions, and provide a detailed estimate. From there, we'll work together to bring your vision to life.
